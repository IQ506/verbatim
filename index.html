About Scott

Scott Hilsee is a commercial‑ready voice talent delivering clear, articulate reads with the polish brands expect. From persuasive explainers and industrial training to confident documentary narration and public‑safety PSAs, Scott’s performance combines precision articulation with a steady, trustworthy tone. Sessions are efficient and collaborative—direction is welcome and turnaround is fast. Based in a broadcast‑quality home studio, Scott records on a Sennheiser MKH 416 and Stellar X2 through a Focusrite Scarlett 2i2, editing in Reaper for clean, consistent delivery. Live sessions are available via Source‑Connect or Zoom, with mastered WAV/AIFF/MP3 delivery and usage‑based licensing on request. Whether you need a composed narrator, an approachable instructor, or a confident spokesperson, Scott focuses on serving the script and elevating the brand.
